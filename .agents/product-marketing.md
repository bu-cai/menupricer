# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-07-20*

## Product Overview

**One-liner:** AI-powered menu pricing tool that tells restaurant owners the right price for every dish in 30 seconds.

**What it does:** MenuPricer takes a dish name and ingredient costs, then uses AI to generate 3 pricing tiers (Budget / Standard / Premium) with profit margin analysis, delivery platform commission calculations, and AI-written menu copy. It can also estimate ingredient costs automatically when you don't have them ("Quick Estimate" mode).

**Product category:** Menu pricing tool / food cost calculator / restaurant management software

**Product type:** SaaS web app (Next.js, hosted at aimenupricer.com)

**Business model:**
- Free: up to 5 menu items, no cloud sync, no PDF export
- Pro: $9/month — unlimited items, cloud sync, PDF export with branding, batch pricing (20 dishes at once), analytics dashboard, dish tags & categories

## Problems & Pain Points

**Core problem:** Restaurant owners price menus by gut feel or competitor copying — resulting in inconsistent margins, unprofitable dishes they don't know about, and no system when costs change.

**Why current solutions fall short:**
- Excel/Google Sheets: time-consuming to build, breaks when updated, no AI
- Pen and paper: no margin calculation, no delivery adjustment
- Restaurant management software (Toast, Square): pricing tools are basic add-ons, not the focus
- Hiring a consultant: expensive ($200+/hr), one-time not ongoing

**What it costs them:**
- A 2% food cost creep = $20,000–$40,000 less profit per year for a mid-volume restaurant
- Hours per week in spreadsheet work
- Pricing decisions delayed when launching new dishes

**Emotional tension:** "I feel like I'm running blind — I know some dishes must be losing money but I don't know which ones." / "I'm afraid to raise prices but I can't keep eating the losses."

## Competitive Landscape

**Direct competitors:**
- piemetrics.co — similar tool, fewer features, smaller reach; MenuPricer has AI cost estimation they don't
- Generic food cost calculators (single-page tools) — no menu management, no AI, no PDF, no cloud sync

**Secondary competitors (different solution, same problem):**
- Excel/Google Sheets — free but manual, no AI, breaks under complexity
- Restaurant POS pricing tools (Toast, Square for Restaurants) — bundled feature, not dedicated

**Indirect competitors:**
- Gut feel / experience — "I've been doing this 20 years, I know my prices"
- Hiring an accountant or consultant — expensive, slow, not real-time

## Differentiation

**Key differentiators:**
1. AI Quick Estimate — type only the dish name, AI estimates ingredient costs automatically (no data entry needed)
2. 3-tier pricing output (Budget / Standard / Premium) — not just one number
3. Delivery platform commission calculator built in (DoorDash, Uber Eats, etc.)
4. AI-written menu copy included with every pricing result
5. Batch pricing — price 20 dishes at once
6. Analytics dashboard — see which dishes have the best/worst margins

**How we do it differently:** Uses AI to eliminate data-entry friction — competitors require full ingredient lists, MenuPricer works with just a dish name.

**Why that's better:** Restaurant owners don't have time for spreadsheets. Speed = adoption.

**Why customers choose us:** "I got a price in 30 seconds without entering a single ingredient." / "It showed me my BBQ platter was only at 48% margin — I fixed it immediately."
